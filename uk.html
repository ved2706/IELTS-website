IELTS tests for UK Visas and Immigration

IELTS Academic , IELTS General Training and IELTS Life Skills are accepted by the UK Visas and immigration (UKV1) as proof of English proficiency for those wishing to live ,work and study in UK.

IELTS tests for UK Visas and immigration are managed by the IELTS Partners, which comprises The British Council, IDP: IELTS Australia and Cambridge English.

IELTS for a Tier 4 Students Visa ( without a pressional )

If you are applying for a Tier 4 Student Visa to enter directly onto a bachelor or postgraduate degree at a university that is a Tier 4 sponsor,you must meet the English language level set by the institution . All british universities and colleges accept IELTS results . Tier 4 Students Visa applications can apply to the UK universities with an IELTS result from more than 1,600 IELTS test locations worldwide . Plese note that some institutions may have additional requirements .

IELTS for UKVI (Academic) delivered on computer .

A limited number of test centres offer the IELTS for UKVI (Academic) on computer. Please note: You can either book IELTS for UKVI online, or you can also download the booking from and register for the test at the test centre.
